### **Insights – Customer Behavior \& Purchase Patterns**



###### **1. Purchase Frequency per Customer**



Insight:

The purchase frequency analysis shows how often each customer makes purchases on the platform. Some customers have significantly higher purchase counts compared to others, indicating a group of highly engaged users. Identifying these frequent buyers can help the business design loyalty programs, targeted promotions, or personalized recommendations to retain valuable customers and increase long-term revenue.



###### **2. Average Invoice Value per Customer**



Insight:

The average invoice value analysis highlights how much customers typically spend per purchase. Customers with higher average purchase values contribute more revenue per transaction, suggesting stronger purchasing power or preference for larger purchases. This insight helps the business identify high-value customers and design strategies such as premium recommendations or bundled offers.



###### **3. Total Spending per Customer**



Insight:

The total spending analysis identifies customers who contribute the most revenue to the store over time. These high-spending customers represent the most valuable segment of the customer base. Recognizing these customers enables the business to focus on customer retention strategies, loyalty rewards, and personalized marketing efforts to maintain strong relationships with top contributors.

###### **6. Customer Most Recent Purchase**



Insight:

The most recent purchase analysis highlights when customers last interacted with the store through a purchase. Customers with more recent purchase dates are considered active users, while those with older purchase dates may be at risk of churn. This insight allows the business to identify inactive customers and design re-engagement strategies such as targeted promotions or personalized offers.
