###### 6\. Genre Revenue Analysis

The analysis indicates that rock-related genres dominate both sales volume and revenue generation. Popular artists such as Queen and Jimi Hendrix contribute significantly to total sales, suggesting that promoting high-performing genres and artists could further increase overall revenue.
